🌱 EcoBot: Your AI Sustainability Concierge
EcoBot is an AI-driven sustainability assistant that helps households and micro-industries bridge the gap between sustainability intent and real-world action. It simplifies complex environmental decisions into personalized, actionable insights that reduce utility costs and carbon impact.

🚨 Problem Statement
Modern households and micro-industries face a Sustainability Gap. While people want to reduce their environmental footprint and save on utility bills, they are overwhelmed by technical jargon, fragmented government schemes, and lack of clear, personalized guidance. Sustainability information is scattered across ministries and manuals, making it inaccessible to most users.

🎯 Target Users
Urban Homeowners – Reduce electricity and water bills; explore solar and rainwater harvesting
Small Businesses / SMEs – Improve energy efficiency and waste management while staying compliant
Environmentally Conscious Youth – Track, visualize, and gamify carbon reduction efforts
💡 Proposed Solution – EcoBot
EcoBot acts as a premium AI sustainability concierge by offering:

Multi-Topic Audit Chatbot – Guided audits for Energy, Water, Waste, and Climate
Dynamic Impact Lab – Real-time CO₂ reduction and cost-saving calculations
Scheme Matcher – Identifies eligibility for government schemes like PM Surya Ghar and UJALA
Pro-Eco Concierge UI – Professional, trust-building interface for sustainability decisions

🧠 Design Thinking Process
Empathy
Interviews with homeowners and small shop owners
Users fear high upfront costs and unclear ROI
Need identified: clear, currency-based savings
Problem Definition
Users need immediate financial clarity because environmental impact alone is not a strong short-term motivator.

Ideation Highlights
Hinglish language support for accessibility
Lazy audits using ranges instead of exact readings
Visual impact cards for instant feedback
🔄 Prototype Flows
Electricity: Bill → Appliances → Roof Potential → Scheme Match → Impact Summary
Water: Usage Estimate → Bill → Infrastructure → Savings Advice
Waste: Sector → Waste Type → Segregation → Protocol
🧪 Testing Strategy
Internal Alpha testing for logic and scheme matching
User Acceptance Testing with Hinglish mode
Accuracy audit using BEE and government data
