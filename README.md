# 🌱 EcoBot – Your AI Sustainability Concierge

EcoBot is an AI-driven sustainability assistant designed to help households and micro-industries transform sustainability goals into practical, cost-saving actions. It simplifies complex environmental decisions into personalized insights that reduce utility expenses and environmental impact.

---

# 🚨 Problem Statement

Modern households and micro-industries face a major **Sustainability Gap**.

Although many people want to:
- reduce electricity and water bills,
- lower their carbon footprint,
- adopt sustainable practices,

they often struggle due to:
- technical jargon,
- scattered government information,
- unclear return on investment (ROI),
- lack of personalized guidance.

Sustainability resources are fragmented across ministries, manuals, and policy documents, making them inaccessible to ordinary users.

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution – EcoBot

EcoBot acts as a premium AI sustainability concierge offering intelligent and actionable sustainability guidance.

## 🔹 Multi-Topic Audit Chatbot
Guided sustainability audits for:
- Energy
- Water
- Waste
- Climate

## 🔹 Dynamic Impact Lab
Real-time calculations for:
- CO₂ reduction
- Utility cost savings
- Environmental impact estimation

## 🔹 Scheme Matcher
Identifies eligibility for government initiatives such as:
- PM Surya Ghar Yojana
- UJALA Scheme
- State-level sustainability incentives

## 🔹 Pro-Eco Concierge UI
A professional and trust-building interface focused on:
- simplicity,
- clarity,
- accessibility,
- actionable insights.

# 🧠 Design Thinking Process

## 🔹 Empathy Stage
Interviews were conducted with:
- homeowners,
- tenants,
- small business owners.

### Key Insights
- Users fear high upfront sustainability costs.
- ROI is often unclear.
- Environmental benefits alone are not strong short-term motivators.
- Users prefer direct monetary savings explanations.

---

## 🔹 Problem Definition

Users need:
- immediate financial clarity,
- simplified sustainability guidance,
- actionable recommendations,
- localized scheme awareness.

---

## 🔹 Ideation Highlights

### 🌐 Accessibility Features
- Hinglish language support
- Simplified conversational flow

### ⚡ Smart Audits
- Lazy audits using estimated ranges
- No requirement for exact technical readings

### 📊 Instant Visual Feedback
- Dynamic impact cards
- Cost-saving visualization
- Carbon reduction indicators
